<div class="bb-page-header">
  <h1>Careers in Biotechnology</h1>
  <p>There's no single path into biotech. Here are the major routes, with realistic entry points.</p>
</div>

<div class="bb-career-card">
<h2>Research</h2>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>What it is</strong>
<p>Discovering new knowledge, developing therapies, studying biological systems</p>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Where</strong>
<p>Universities, research institutes, pharma companies, government labs</p>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Entry points</strong>
<ul>
<li>Lab technician (Bachelor's degree)</li>
<li>Research associate (Bachelor's + experience)</li>
<li>PhD scientist</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Skills needed</strong>
<p>Experimental design, data analysis, patience, curiosity</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="bb-career-card">
<h2>Industry / Manufacturing</h2>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>What it is</strong>
<p>Scaling up production, quality control, process optimization</p>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Where</strong>
<p>Biotech companies, pharmaceutical manufacturers, contract manufacturing organizations (CMOs)</p>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Entry points</strong>
<ul>
<li>Manufacturing associate (High school + training)</li>
<li>Process engineer (Bachelor's in engineering)</li>
<li>Quality assurance specialist (Bachelor's in science)</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Skills needed</strong>
<p>Attention to detail, problem-solving, regulatory knowledge</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="bb-career-card">
<h2>Clinical</h2>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>What it is</strong>
<p>Testing therapies in humans, managing trials, patient safety</p>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Where</strong>
<p>Hospitals, clinical research organizations (CROs), pharma companies</p>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Entry points</strong>
<ul>
<li>Clinical research coordinator (Bachelor's)</li>
<li>Clinical trial manager (Bachelor's + experience)</li>
<li>Medical science liaison (Advanced degree)</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Skills needed</strong>
<p>Organization, communication, ethics, regulatory compliance</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="bb-career-card">
<h2>Regulatory / Policy</h2>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>What it is</strong>
<p>Navigating FDA approval, compliance, public health policy</p>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Where</strong>
<p>Government agencies (FDA, NIH), consulting firms, pharma companies</p>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Entry points</strong>
<ul>
<li>Regulatory affairs specialist (Bachelor's)</li>
<li>Policy analyst (Bachelor's in science/policy)</li>
<li>Compliance officer (Bachelor's + certifications)</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Skills needed</strong>
<p>Writing, attention to detail, understanding regulations</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="bb-career-card">
<h2>Business / Operations</h2>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>What it is</strong>
<p>Strategy, sales, partnerships, operations, project management</p>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Where</strong>
<p>Biotech companies, consulting firms, venture capital, startups</p>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Entry points</strong>
<ul>
<li>Business analyst (Bachelor's in business/science)</li>
<li>Project manager (Bachelor's + experience)</li>
<li>Sales representative (Bachelor's)</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Skills needed</strong>
<p>Communication, business acumen, networking</p>
</div>
</div>

<div class="bb-career-card">
<h2>Science Communication</h2>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>What it is</strong>
<p>Translating science for public audiences, education, journalism</p>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Where</strong>
<p>Media outlets, nonprofits, museums, biotech companies</p>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Entry points</strong>
<ul>
<li>Science writer (Bachelor's + writing portfolio)</li>
<li>Public engagement coordinator (Bachelor's)</li>
<li>Medical communications specialist (Bachelor's + experience)</li>
</ul>
</div>
<div class="bb-career-detail">
<strong>Skills needed</strong>
<p>Writing, storytelling, scientific literacy</p>
</div>
</div>
