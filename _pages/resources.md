# Curated Resources for Biotech Beginners

All resources are free (or have free tiers) and beginner-friendly.

---

## Newsletters

- **[The Readout](https://www.statnews.com/newsletters/)** - STAT News weekly biotech/pharma roundup
- **[Endpoints News](https://endpts.com/)** - Biotech industry news and analysis
- **[Nature Briefing](https://www.nature.com/briefing)** - Daily science news from Nature

---

## Podcasts

- **[The Biotech Startups Podcast](https://biotechstartups.com/podcast/)** - Founder stories and industry insights
- **[Mendelspod](https://mendelspod.com/)** - Science communication and genetics
- **[STAT's "The Readout Loud"](https://www.statnews.com/category/readout-loud/)** - Weekly biotech news discussion

---

## Beginner-Friendly Reads

- **[Cells Are Very Small](https://www.alextelford.com/cells-are-very-small)** - Visual intro to cell biology
- **[Learn.Genetics](https://learn.genetics.utah.edu/)** - Interactive genetics tutorials
- **[Scitable by Nature](https://www.nature.com/scitable)** - Free science education resource

---

## YouTube Channels

- **[Kurzgesagt - Genetic Engineering](https://www.youtube.com/c/inanutshell)** - Animated science explainers
- **[Primer](https://www.youtube.com/c/PrimerBlobs)** - Evolution and biology simulations
- **[Veritasium](https://www.youtube.com/c/veritasium)** - Science and engineering

---

## Online Courses (Free)

- **[MIT OpenCourseWare - Biology](https://ocw.mit.edu/courses/biology/)** - Full MIT courses, free
- **[Khan Academy - Biology](https://www.khanacademy.org/science/biology)** - Comprehensive, self-paced
- **[iBiology](https://www.ibiology.org/)** - Talks by leading scientists

---

## Books (Accessible)

- **"The Gene" by Siddhartha Mukherjee** - History of genetics
- **"The Immortal Life of Henrietta Lacks"** - Ethics and cell biology
- **"Spillover" by David Quamman** - Infectious diseases and ecology
