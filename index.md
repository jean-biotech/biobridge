## Your Bridge to Biotech Starts Here

BioBridge exists because biotech often feels overly technical, credential-gated, and unwelcoming. We lower the barrier to entry through plain-language education, mentorship, and clear pathways.

**You don't need a science background. You just need curiosity.**
